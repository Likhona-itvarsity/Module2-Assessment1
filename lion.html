LION

Lions are majestic creatures that are native to Africa and India. They are the second-largest living cat after the tiger. Lions have a muscular, broad-chested body; short, rounded head; round ears; and a hairy tuft at the end of their tail. Adult male lions are larger than females and have a prominent mane. Groups of female lions usually hunt together, preying mostly on large ungulates.
